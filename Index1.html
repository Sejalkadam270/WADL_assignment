Grilled Salmon Salad

Ingredients: Salmon, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olive oil, lemon, salt, and pepper.

Instructions: Grill the salmon until cooked, mix with the greens, tomatoes, and cucumber. Drizzle with olive oil, squeeze lemon, and season with salt and pepper.
